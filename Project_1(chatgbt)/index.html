Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers in history. He began his career at FC Barcelona, where he spent over 20 years, winning numerous trophies, including 10 La Liga titles and 4 UEFA Champions League titles. Known for his extraordinary dribbling, vision, and finishing, Messi has won seven Ballon d'Or awards. In 2021, he joined Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) and later helped Argentina win the FIFA World Cup in 2022. Messi is also celebrated for his humility and philanthropy, supporting children's health and education through his foundation.
